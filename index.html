Research

I mainly work on planning and control for soft robots trying to combine model-based and learning-based approaches. I strongly believe that there are some things worth learning and other things have been known for years.
My goal is to exploit the use of the robot's model to generate explosive motions and increase the robustness by learning while dwrawing theoretical proofs.

Mission

Our mission is to impact industrial monitoring and inspection through end-to-end robotic solutions. We are committed to providing solutions that minimize human risk, reduce costs, and increase sustainability.

Vision

We envision a future where robots handle dangerous and tedious tasks, freeing humans to focus on higher-value work. By bridging natural and artificial intelligence, we aim to create safer, smarter, and more sustainable environments, driving efficiency and reliability across industries.

Background

Expert team of robotic engineering and automation from @UNIPI. In particular, EU H2020 project, Natural Intelligence (NI) 101016970, we develop frameworks for planning autonomous missions aimed at data collection and the control and autonomous locomotion of floating base robots.
